Your Privacy & Cookie Preferences

At Labeo Tech, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website to enhance your experience.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To remember your preferences and settings.
  • To provide personalized content and ads.
  • To analyze website traffic and improve our website's performance.
  • To improve user experience on our site.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the basic functioning of our website. Without these cookies, the website will not work as intended.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies help us analyze how users interact with our website, which allows us to improve its performance.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies remember your preferences to improve your experience on our website (e.g., language preference).
  • Targeting/Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to provide personalized ads and measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.

How to Control Cookies

You can control or disable cookies by adjusting your browser settings. However, please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website. Here's how you can manage cookies in common browsers:

  • Chrome: Go to Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data.
  • Firefox: Go to Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data.
  • Safari: Go to Preferences → Privacy → Block all cookies.
  • Edge: Go to Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Cookies and site permissions.
